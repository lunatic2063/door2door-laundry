📋 Terms & Conditions
Please read before booking.

1. Service Area

Door2Door Laundry Co. currently serves Fort Wayne and New Haven, IN. Pickup and delivery are available Monday, Wednesday, and Friday between 7:00 AM and 11:00 AM only. We will confirm your booking via text or email.

2. Bag Requirements

All laundry must be placed in a standard 13-gallon trash bag. Bags should not be torn or damaged. If a bag is overstuffed beyond the standard 13-gallon capacity, the excess weight will be charged at $2.00 per pound in addition to the flat rate. We will weigh the excess and notify you of any additional charges before processing.

3. Special Items

Baby clothes must be placed in a separate smaller bag inside the 13-gallon bag and clearly identified. Stained items must be placed in a separate smaller bag inside the 13-gallon bag. Failure to separate items as instructed may result in items being washed together without pretreatment or Dreft.

4. Pockets & Personal Items

Door2Door Laundry Co. is not responsible for any items left in pockets, including but not limited to cash, cards, jewelry, electronics, or personal belongings. Please check all pockets before placing items in your bag.

5. Pre-Existing Damage

Door2Door Laundry Co. is not responsible for pre-existing damage to clothing or linens, including but not limited to fading, shrinking of items that are not labeled for machine washing, loose buttons, broken zippers, or fabric wear. It is the customer's responsibility to identify delicate or special-care items.

6. Turnaround Time

Standard turnaround is 24–48 hours. We will notify you when your laundry is ready for delivery. Delays due to weather, high volume, or other unforeseen circumstances will be communicated as soon as possible.

7. Payment

First-time customers are required to pay in full before pickup to confirm their booking. Returning customers in good standing may pay at the time of delivery. Accepted payment methods include Square (Apple Pay, Google Pay, all major credit/debit cards), Venmo (@Door2DoorLaundry), Cash App ($Door2DoorLaundry), Zelle (269-267-6508), and cash.

8. Late Payment & Non-Payment

Payment not received within 24 hours of delivery will incur a $5.00 late fee. Payment not received within 48 hours may result in immediate suspension of service until the full balance including late fees is paid. Repeated non-payment will result in permanent termination of service. Door2Door Laundry Co. reserves the right to pursue unpaid balances through small claims court. All deliveries are documented with a timestamped photo as proof of delivery.

9. Cancellations

Please cancel or reschedule at least 12 hours before your scheduled pickup by texting or calling 269-267-6508. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a cancellation fee.

10. Lost Items

Door2Door Laundry Co. takes great care with every load. In the rare event an item cannot be located, we will make every reasonable effort to find it. We are not liable for items that cannot be accounted for due to improper bagging or unlabeled bags with multiple customers.

11. Allergen & Detergent Disclosure

Door2Door Laundry Co. uses the following detergents: Tide (Original), Tide Free & Clear, and Ecos Natural. Customers with known sensitivities or allergies to any of these products must disclose this information at the time of booking. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment as detergents may vary between loads. Door2Door Laundry Co. is not liable for allergic reactions resulting from failure to disclose known sensitivities.

Fabric Softener Beads: Contain fragrance. Customers with fragrance sensitivities should not select this option.

Dreft (Baby Clothes): While Dreft is formulated for sensitive baby skin, parents should verify that their child has no known sensitivity to Dreft ingredients before selecting this option. Door2Door Laundry Co. is not liable for reactions resulting from undisclosed sensitivities.

12. Biohazardous Materials — Strictly Prohibited

Door2Door Laundry Co. does not accept any fabrics, garments, or items contaminated with biohazardous materials. This includes but is not limited to: blood, bodily fluids, fecal matter, vomit, medical waste, or any materials that pose a biological health risk. Submission of biohazardous items is a violation of these Terms & Conditions and may result in immediate termination of service, return of the bag unopened, and forfeiture of any fees paid. We reserve the right to report any biohazardous submissions to the appropriate authorities.

13. Right to Refuse Service

Door2Door Laundry Co. reserves the right to refuse service for items that are excessively soiled beyond normal laundry, items containing biohazardous materials, items that pose a health or safety risk, or bags that do not meet our requirements. A refusal of service does not guarantee a refund of any fees already paid.

14. Privacy

Your personal information (name, address, phone, email) is used solely to fulfill your laundry service. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

15. Agreement

By submitting a pickup request, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these Terms & Conditions, including the allergen disclosure and biohazardous materials policy. Door2Door Laundry Co. reserves the right to update these terms at any time.
